About

A benefit initiative that turns art into direct humanitarian support for Venezuela.

We hope you're doing well. We're reaching out to invite you to take part in Miami Art Fair for Venezuela, a benefit pop-up art fair organized by Violette Bule and Nicolás Gerardi, hosted by La Madre – Asulprusia, Miami.

Why we're doing this

Venezuela has been devastated by a catastrophic earthquake, worsening an already severe humanitarian crisis. Thousands of families have lost their homes, hospitals are overwhelmed, and access to medicine, medical equipment, electricity, and clean water remains critically limited.

We are deeply grateful to La Madre (@eliechemendia & @ioseoablo) and @Asulprusia for opening their doors and supporting this initiative.

The fair

Artists are invited to donate artworks, artist books, zines, or fanzines to help raise funds for emergency medical relief.

  • DatesSaturday, September 5 & Sunday, September 6 · 6:00–9:00 PM
  • LocationLa Madre, 1657 N. Miami Ave., Apt. 814, Miami, FL

Where the money goes

100% of proceeds will go directly toward purchasing and delivering urgently needed medicines, medical supplies, and equipment to hospitals and healthcare workers in Venezuela. We are committed to transparency and will share updates throughout the campaign.

Take part

If you would like to participate, please complete the submission form: artist submission form ↗. We will follow up with details about artwork delivery, promotion, and the fundraising timeline. Please share this invitation with other artists who may be interested.

For questions, write to artistsforvenezuelamiami@gmail.com.
